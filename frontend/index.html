The Growing Threat

AI-driven fraud is escalating at an alarming rate

AI Voice Cloning

Scammers now use advanced AI tools to clone real human voices. These systems can sound exactly like a family member, friend, or trusted authority. Instead of making one fake call at a time, scammers can run thousands of automated calls simultaneously, each sounding completely real and natural.

Massive Financial Losses

Because the voices sound convincing, the usual warning signs are disappearing. In India alone, people have lost around ₹475 Crores. Globally, losses exceed ₹22,845 Crores annually. A single successful call can steal between ₹20,000 and ₹1 Lakh from a victim's bank account.

Vulnerable Targets

About 1 in 4 people are being targeted. These scams especially affect elderly individuals and people in rural areas, who tend to trust phone calls from someone they believe is a loved one or an official authority.

Our Solution

Defense against the active threat of AI-driven fraud

Voice Authentication

Our AI-powered defender instantly differentiates between authentic human vocal cords and computer-generated synthetic audio, catching microscopic digital artifacts invisible to the human ear.

Behavioral & Intent Analysis

Even if the caller is human, the system cross-analyzes real-time psychological patterns such as false urgency, emotional coercion, or specific payment demands to flag social engineering attempts.

Restoring Digital Trust

Operating as a real-time filter, our solution provides clear, immediate warnings before the damage is done, ensuring people never have to fall prey to such scams.
